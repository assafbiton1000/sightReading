Privacy Policy for FreePace Piano

Last updated: July 13, 2026

FreePace Piano ("the app", "we", "our") is a piano sight-reading practice app. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and what choices you have.

1. Microphone Access

The app requests microphone access to detect the pitch of notes you play on a real piano, so it can check your practice in real time. This audio is analyzed entirely on your device using a local pitch-detection algorithm running inside the app. Raw audio is never recorded, saved, uploaded, or transmitted anywhere — it exists only in memory for the fraction of a second needed to detect a pitch, and is then discarded. We (and no third party) ever receive or have access to this audio.

If you prefer not to grant microphone access, the app offers an on-screen piano keyboard as an alternative input method that requires no microphone at all.

2. Account Information (optional)

Creating an account is optional. If you choose to sign up (by email/password or "Continue with Google"), we collect and store the following on our backend infrastructure (provided by Supabase):

  • Your email address
  • Your display name
  • Your account creation date
  • Your password, which is hashed and never stored or visible in plain text (email/password sign-up only)

This information is used solely to authenticate you and let you access your account. We do not use your email for marketing and do not share it with advertisers.

If you sign in with Google, Google shares your email address and basic profile information with our authentication provider under Google's own privacy policy; we do not receive your Google password.

3. Data Stored Only on Your Device

Your practice history, streaks, accuracy statistics, and app settings (difficulty level, sound, language, dark mode, etc.) are stored locally on your device only and are never uploaded to any server. If you sign out or delete your account, this local data remains on your device and is only removed if you uninstall the app or clear its storage.

4. Notifications

If you enable the daily practice reminder, it is scheduled locally on your device using your device's notification system. No data about your reminder preferences leaves your device.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not use advertising networks or third-party analytics/tracking SDKs in this app. The only third-party service involved is Supabase, which securely hosts the optional account system described above, and Google, solely if you choose "Continue with Google" to sign in.

6. Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your account at any time from the Profile screen. This immediately and permanently removes your account record (email, name, password) from our backend. It does not affect practice data already stored locally on your device.

7. Children's Privacy

FreePace Piano is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Account creation is optional and requires an email address.

8. Security

Account data is stored and transmitted using industry-standard encryption provided by our backend infrastructure provider (Supabase). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
