## Full description (max 4000 chars)

Learn to read music by actually playing it. FreePace Piano listens to your real
piano through your phone's microphone and follows your practice note by note —
no forced tempo, no metronome, no waiting. Play at whatever speed feels right,
and the app keeps up with you.

**How it works**
Pick a difficulty level, choose treble clef, bass clef, or both, and start
playing. The app detects each note you play in real time and only moves
forward once you've played the right one — so you can take your time on a
tricky passage without being rushed.

**Key features**
• Free-tempo practice — the app follows your playing, not the other way around
• Real-time pitch detection from your piano through the microphone
• 8 progressively harder levels — note range, key signatures, chords, and
  rhythms all build up gradually
• Treble and bass clef, including two-hand exercises
• A small built-in song library to sight-read real, familiar tunes
• "Disappearing measures" mode — notes fade away as you play them, to train
  reading ahead and memory
• No microphone? Use the built-in on-screen piano keyboard instead
• After-the-fact rhythm feedback — see exactly where your timing drifted,
  without being interrupted mid-performance
• Practice history, streaks, and accuracy stats to track your progress
• Optional daily practice reminder
• Light and dark mode
• Available in 9 languages: English, Hebrew, Arabic, Russian, French, German,
  Spanish, Italian, and Portuguese

Whether you're a beginner learning your first notes or working your way up to
faster and more complex pieces, FreePace Piano turns your own piano into a
patient, note-perfect practice partner.
